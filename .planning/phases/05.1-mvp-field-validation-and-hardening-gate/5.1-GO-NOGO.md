# 5.1 Go / No-Go Decision

**Phase:** 05.1 — MVP Field Validation and Hardening Gate  
**Date:** 2026-07-06  
**Decision:** **Go — Android phone MVP validated for Phase 6 planning**

## Scope

This decision applies to the mounted-phone Android MVP. It does not certify iOS
real-device behavior and does not certify external GNSS, glasses, satellite maps,
map ghost rendering, or AI driving analysis.

Phase 6 may begin from the Android phone source-of-truth app. iOS remains an
explicit unvalidated risk.

## Rationale

The Android mounted-phone MVP has passed the automated verification gate, local
device deployment gate, launch smoke gate, and owner field-UAT acceptance gate.
Known Phase 5.1 blockers discovered in field testing have been addressed or
moved to backlog where they are not required for the Android phone MVP gate.

The original Plan 07 five-session evidence package is not fully archived in this
repository. This decision treats that as an owner-accepted evidence caveat rather
than a timing-path No-Go. Future precision work, especially external GNSS, should
restore the stricter replay/video artifact discipline.

## Unlocks

- Phase 6 planning and implementation may begin for external GNSS / sensor
  ingestion, Android-first.
- Phase 7 remains conceptually unlocked only after the phone app continues to
  produce reliable timing state from Phase 6 inputs; do not start glasses before
  the phone source-of-truth bridge is stable.

## Residual Risks

- iOS real-device UAT is still not complete.
- Real higher-speed track sessions with preserved JSON/GPX/video replay evidence
  should be collected before claiming timing precision beyond the phone GPS MVP.
- App license policy remains an open product decision.

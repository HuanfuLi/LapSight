Deliver the isolated, high-uncertainty track-extraction algorithm: a pure shared service that converts a continuous marking run into a reference line by finding repeated spatial structure, scoring loops, rejecting outliers, and averaging accepted loops — plus the Track Review domain state that wraps its result.

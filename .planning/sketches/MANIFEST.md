# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction

LapSight is a mounted-phone timing instrument entering a product-polish phase.
The pre-timing Drive surface should feel deliberate, stable, and glanceable:
one clear track-center entry, one restrained local map, progressive disclosure
for optional glasses, and one state-correct primary action. Timing remains an
exclusive fullscreen surface. Track discovery and management live in a
fullscreen, list-first Track Center; performance data stays in Session Review.

## Reference Points

- Native iOS information hierarchy and touch targets
- Motorsport instrumentation: high contrast, restrained chrome, numeric clarity
- Navigation apps: a manipulable map is not also a full-surface navigation button
